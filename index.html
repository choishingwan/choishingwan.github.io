Personal Statement

I am a Postdoctoral Research Worker working under Dr Paul F. O'Reilly at the MRC SGDP Centre, Institute of Psychiatry, King's College London. I did my PhD at the University of Hong Kong (2012-16) supervised by Professor Pak Chung Sham and co-supervised by Dr Stacey S. Cherny and Dr Wanling Yang. Currently I am focusing on updating PRSice 2 and am also working on methods related to inversion detection in GWAS data

Methods and Softwares

  • PRSice: Re-implemented PRSice v1.25 in C++ to improve its speed and functionality. Also implemented the PRSet and PRSlice function. For detail, please refer to our wiki

  • GreedyRelated: A simple program for optimize selection of samples when removing related samples from a GWAS study

Dissertation

Title

The Genetics of Schizophrenia - The Contribution of Common Variants and Genes Implicated in a Maternal Immune Activation Model
Primary Supervisor

Professor Pak-Chung SHAM

My study aim to develop algorithm for the estimation of narrow sense heritability using only test statistics and to identify genetic factors contributing to

  1. schizophrenia susceptibility using RNA Sequencing;

  2. antipsychotic treatment response outcome using GWAS
